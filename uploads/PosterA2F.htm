Which is the innovation?

Marugame Udon Indonesia’s innovation is its integration of sustainability into every part of the business. By aligning operations with the SDGs creating jobs, promoting education and gender equality, reducing waste, and supporting ethical farming, it turns a restaurant chain into a model of responsible growth that benefits people, communities, and the environment.
